This is a book, or catalog, that highlights many of my design influences. These influences range from artists and designers to places, people, objects, and ideas. Each section has its own visual system and I intentionally left out page numbers to reflect the way I organize these influences in my head more visually than logically. I also doodled throughout the book as that is something I’m constantly doing and I find has an influence on my art and design.

Above you’ll find some key spreads and a brief video to get a sense of the book, to view the entire book visit issuu.com.
