This first semester senior year project was an introduction to a potential thesis idea I can pursue in my second semester of senior year. The format for the book was to create an A-Z Lexicon as a way of exploring this potential thesis idea. I chose my A-Z to be everyday objects and nature I find around me that I believe can be used for inspiration in design. I expand on this idea on the fourth slide that has my work in progress thesis statement. To see a full digital version of this book visit issuu.com. For more about the process of making this book visit my medium blog post.
