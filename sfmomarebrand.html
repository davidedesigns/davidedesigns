This project consisted in redesigning/rebranding the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art. Their current logo and visual system is ctually quite new and is meant to be more welcoming and open. For the redesign I wanted to add energy and excitement to the museums visual system. I wanted to create something very different than what they have now and very distinct from the New York MoMA.

The line motif captures the hilly geographyÂ of San Francisco as well as the energy mentioned before. The logo itself was inspired by the angular shape of the original Mario Botta building and represents the contrast between the incredible outside architecture and inside art.
