This is a triptych I submitted to the BU Arts Initiative which got selected to be displayed in the BU Arts Initiative office windows in the George Sherman Union building. The piece represents the way I feel my brain works when I create art; a free flow of thoughts and ideas. I made this using a combination of adobe illustrator and pen and ink.
