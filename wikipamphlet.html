In this mini project I selected a wikipedia article to create a short pamphlet for. The article I began with was about samurai which then led me to an article on katanas, which in turn led me to an article on pattern welding. Pattern welding is a technique used in some swordsmithing that mixes several pieces of different metal that are welded and twisted together resulting in a unique pattern that I referenced on the cover and internal pages. To see a digital, flippable version of the pamphlet visit issuu.com.
