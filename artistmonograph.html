This was a project where I selected an artist to create a short monograph for. The text used in this is dummy text and above are only a select number of spreads from the whole book. To view the entire book visit issuu.com. The artist I selected was Katerina Marcelja who is a Brooklyn based artist that does primarly installation and sculpture based work. Her style is quite peculiar and she uses the strangeness of her work to engage the viewer. Because her work is usually installed in very empty and stark spaces I wanted this book to similarly use as much white space as possible. I also wanted the feel of the book to match the strange feel of her work. I decided to achieve this through my typeface choices where I used very unusual fonts to enhance the viewing and learning experience of her work.
